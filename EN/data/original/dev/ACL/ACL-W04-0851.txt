Regularized Least-Squares Classification For Word Sense Disambiguation .
The paper describes RLSC-LIN and RLSCCOMB systems which participated in the Senseval-3 English lexical sample task .
These systems are based on Regularized Least-Squares Classification ( RLSC ) learning method .
We describe the reasons of choosing this method , how we applied it to word sense disambiguation , what results we obtained on Senseval-1 , Senseval-2 and Senseval-3 data and discuss some possible improvements .
