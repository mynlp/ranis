Building Parallel Corpora For eContent Professionals .
This paper reports on completed work carried out in the framework of the INTERA project , and specifically , on the production of multilingual resources ( LRs ) for eContent purposes .
The paper presents the methodology adopted for the development of the corpus ( acquisition and processing of the textual data ) , discusses the divergence of the initial assumptions from the actual situation met during this procedure , and concludes with a summarization of the problems attested which undermine the viability of multilingual parallel corpora construction .
